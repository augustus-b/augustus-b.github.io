Who We Are

We are a group of young individuals born and raised in the remote places of the Philippines. Growing up, we've had access to quality resources. However, we have a lot of childhood friends who didn't because of poverty. We've come to realize that not everyone receives the same opportunities as we have and there are still a lot of children out there denied of achieving their dreams.

We believe that empowering the youth by granting them access to quality opportunities and on-demand skills could make a whole difference in molding a better community.

Empathy at Work

People who really make change happen are often those who can effectively think outside of their own experiences, and connect with others on a deeper level. By beginning to view life as interconnected, we can all live in harmony and with high overall well-being.

Empowering Youth for a Better Community

Teen.able believes in the power and potential of young Filipinos to be catalysts for positive change in their community. Driven by this belief, our mission is to empower Filipino teens through specialized skills development.

Transforming the Self and Society

Change starts with oneself. Change in mentality, habits, and actions makes it possible to transform our surroundings. It can be difficult, however the fight for change can lead to a new (and better) normal for us to enjoy.
